About Rebecca

Rebecca is a web developer from South-West Scotland. Graduating with a degree in law in 2019, she worked in the legal
field for a short time, before discovering her love for design and branding. From there, she undertook a career change with
ITCareerSwitch in 2024 and plans to start her own web development company in 2025.

Rebecca combines creativity with a flare for critical thinking, ensuring that her clients get the very best from their branding
and websites.

She currently lives in rural Wigtownshire with her black lab, Pippin.
