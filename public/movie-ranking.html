The Best Movies According to Rebecca

My Top Three Movies of All Time

You've Got Mail

This movie reminds me of autumn in New York, and is the ultimate comfort movie. I always watch it in September to kick of my seasonal flicks. For maximum enjoyment, add homemade cinnamon buns or Chai cookies.

Interstellar

The cinematic value of this film is immense. It is visually beautiful and the storyline is powerful. Emotive, devastating, heart-wrenching. The acting performaces are fantastic from young to seasoned actors playing characters with incredible depth, range, and committment.

Promising Young Woman

This movie is empowering, devastating, gut-wrenching, and just about everything in between. With performaces from Carey Mulligan and Bo Burnham to name just a couple, the acting depth and range is inescapable and poignant. Everything about this movie is jaw-dropping.
